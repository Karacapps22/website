Things I want to do

• Encourage Computer Science and Engineering in schools (virtually and in person!)
- Many students in highschool and middle school aren't given the opportunity to learn about Computer Science and Engineering.
- A study showed that students who are told that they would be good at Computer Science are 2.5 to 3 times more likely to be interested in it. Additionally, the study's research reports that students who have high self confidence in their ability to learn computer science are nearly four times more interested in learning computer science.
- By encouraging younger students and being role models, we can give them the opportunity to pursue Computer Science and Engineering
